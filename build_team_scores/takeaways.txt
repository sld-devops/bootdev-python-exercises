Bugs caught along the way:
1. Checking `team not in plays` instead of `team not in teams_total_pts`
2. `return` was inside the loop — exited too early ()
3. `=+` instead of `+=`

Concepts learned:
- Why you unpack both `team, score` from each tuple
- How `if team not in dict` initializes a key only once
- `+=` adds to existing value, `=+` just assigns
- `return` must be outside the loop to get the full result
